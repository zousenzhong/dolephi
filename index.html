About Us

A Factory You Can Trust at Scale

Since 2009, Dolephi has specialized in designing and manufacturing high-quality shower enclosures for B2B partners worldwide. From our 35,000 m2 facility, we produce over 200,000 units annually -- serving distributors, hotel groups, contractors, and retailers across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia.

  • In-house production -- glass cutting, tempering, aluminum extrusion, and assembly under one roof for full quality control.
  • OEM/ODM expertise -- from concept design to packaging, we build products to your brand specification.
  • Global compliance -- CE, UPC, CUPC, WaterMark certified. Tempered glass meets EN 12150 and ANSI Z97.1 standards.
  • Reliable lead times -- 25-35 days standard. 99.2% on-time delivery rate tracked over 12 months.

Consumer Pain Points Solved

Real Complaints from Social Media? We Solved Them.

We analyzed thousands of consumer reviews on Facebook, YouTube, and Instagram. Here are the top shower enclosure complaints -- and how Dolephi engineers them away. Fewer complaints means fewer returns for your business.

Facebook YouTube Instagram

Glass spontaneous explosion

YouTube review / Facebook group
My shower glass just shattered out of nowhere in the middle of the night, scared the whole family.
Our solution
EN 12150 certified tempered glass with self-explosion rate below 0.3%. Optional laminated safety film (SGS) that holds fragments even if broken. Every pane laser-etched with tempering date for traceability.

Sliding door jumps off track

Facebook DIY group / YouTube
The sliding door keeps derailing and it is impossible to roll smoothly after 3 months.
Our solution
Premium sealed-bearing rollers tested 100,000+ open-close cycles. Anti-jump derailment design with dual-wheel stabilizers. Smooth glide guaranteed for 10+ years of daily use.

Water leaks from the bottom

Instagram bathroom page / Facebook
Water seeps out under the door every shower, ruined my bathroom floor within weeks.
Our solution
Full-length magnetic seal strip + flexible bottom sweep blade. Every unit 100% water-pressure tested before packaging. Zero-leakage design validated in our spray-test lab.

Hardware rusts within months

YouTube review / Amazon comments
The hinges and handles started rusting after 6 months. Looks terrible and feels cheap.
Our solution
All hardware in solid 304 stainless steel, passivated and salt-spray tested 48+ hours. Chrome and brushed-nickel PVD finishes available. Zero rust, zero corrosion, guaranteed.

Hard water stains impossible to clean

Instagram cleaning hacks / Facebook
Limescale builds up so fast, I spend 20 minutes scrubbing every week. So frustrating.
Our solution
Optional nano-coating Easy-Clean glass surface (hydrophobic). Water beads off, limescale cannot bond. Wipe with a squeegee in 10 seconds. Coating lasts 5+ years.

Installation is a nightmare

YouTube DIY / Facebook contractor group
Instructions made no sense, parts were not labeled, took 4 hours and two trips to the hardware store.
Our solution
Pre-assembled modules with numbered parts. Step-by-step video guides per model (QR code on box). Wall-anchor template included. Average install time: 45 minutes, one person.
