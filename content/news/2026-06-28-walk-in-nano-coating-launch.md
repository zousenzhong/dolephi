## Next-Generation Walk-In Enclosure Now Available

Dolephi is proud to announce the launch of our **Premium Walk-In Enclosure Series (WIE-2026)**, featuring an advanced hydrophobic nano-coating technology that repels water and prevents limescale buildup.

### Key Features

- **Nano-coating glass surface** — water beads off effortlessly, cleaning time reduced by 80%
- **10mm/12mm tempered glass** — EN 12150 certified, self-explosion rate below 0.3%
- **Frameless minimalist design** — ideal for high-end residential and hotel projects
- **304 stainless steel hardware** — salt-spray tested 48+ hours, zero rust guarantee

Our nano-coating directly addresses this — meaning **fewer customer complaints and lower return rates** for distributors and retailers.
