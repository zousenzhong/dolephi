## Capacity Doubled to Meet Global Demand

Dolephi has officially opened its **new 15,000 m2 production facility** adjacent to our existing factory in Foshan, Guangdong. This expansion brings our total production area to **50,000 m2** and increases annual output capacity to **300,000+ units**.

### What's New

1. **Dedicated tempering line** — second EN 12150 certified furnace, doubling glass processing capacity
2. **Automated quality inspection** — AI-powered dimension and defect scanning on 100% of units
3. **Expanded packaging area** — new drop-test station ensuring export-grade packaging
4. **R&D prototyping lab** — dedicated space for OEM/ODM product development and testing

### What This Means for Our Partners

- **Shorter lead times** — standard orders now ship in 20-30 days (down from 25-35)
- **Higher capacity** — accept larger orders without quality compromise
- **Faster prototyping** — ODM samples ready in 7-10 days (down from 15-20)
- **Consistent quality** — automated inspection eliminates human error

### Industry Recognition

The expansion was completed with full compliance to ISO 9001:2015 standards and has been audited by SGS. Our new facility is now ready for customer factory visits — virtual tours available on request.
